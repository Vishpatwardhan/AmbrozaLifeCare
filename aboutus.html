About Us



Ambroza Lifecare LLP is a Mumbai based pharmaceutical company, established in 2015 by Narindra Sharma and Hiral Shah with the vision of making quality medicines affordable for all. Ambroza is a research driven pharmaceutical company with expertise in molecule development. Since its inception in 2015, Ambroza Lifecare LLP is focused to manufacture an extensive spectrum of pharmaceutical formulation products. We engage in the research, development, manufacturing, sourcing, marketing, and distribution of high-quality pharmaceutical products. We are a fully integrated company with in-house business development, RnD and regulatory compliance capabilities.
